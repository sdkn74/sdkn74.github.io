2026-05-11 (The ground be suffering)

We begin in Enoch on Pumpkin Hill. Jack and Sketch approach the town and see tons of farms around us. As they make their way into the village they notice pumpkin lanterns and corn silk garland vendors all around. Jack becomes self-conscious seeing all the harvest born working the fields. Andy and Tenebracanthos enter the town and are drawn to the church of the Patient Lady. While wandering Jack notices a farm with no crops, which hits just a little too close to home with his family's farm. As Jack and Sketch talk to William (the farmer) they hear the whole storey. William says the harvest was going well until it wasn't. Trying to determine what was going on here, and wondering if it was the same as his farm, Jack cast detect magic. He is getting a weird dark magic feeling, similar to the farm he left.

Woody walks up to the Patient Lady stall and hears a woman named Martha asking about her missing son. He asks about Martha's missing son and Sister Rain says that Martha is freaking out over nothing. As the conversation continues Sister Rain mentions that the Patient Lady specializes in new souls (Woody is newer to the world being, at most, a few years old). As Woody and Tenebracanthos are leaving the tent they hear someone say the woods are dangerous. Tenebracanthos is looking for a druid (Tuathal) that is already dead (not killed by Tenebracanthos, been dead for 78 years). As they left the tent they stumbled upon a cat man wearing a bolo tie named Mathosala who seemed to be the town's mayor. While chatting with Mathosala, Tenebracanthos and Woody hear "the undead belong in the woods".

Woody takes interest in going into the Patient Lady's circle in the woods to find Andi. While Tenebracanthos and Woody wander around the square, they run into Jack and Sketch. We all hear a strange voice from a purple tent calling out to Tenebracanthos to get a fortune read. Tenebracanthos crouches into the tent to see Moriz, the tarot reader with a wide brimmed het. A silver is paid and Tenebracanthos get's a fortune, the devil card. "Chained things usually stay chained for a reason". Woody gets "The Fool" card, Jack gets "The Hermit" card, and Sketch gets "The Judgement" card which happens to have a vampire on it.

Tenebracanthos ends up getting interested in the greased pig wrestling. A harvest born named Bill fails to catch the pig and Tenebracanthos goes to try after getting offered the opportunity to go into the pit. While all this is happening Jack talks to the garlic farmer. They have two theories about William's farm. One, William is desperate for friendship and did this to gain sympathy and poisoned his own farm. Two, the butcher and William had a fight and the butcher could have been someone who cast a curse upon his farm. After hearing this information Jack sees the butcher and keeps his distance. We see a bunch of eyes up at another farm and hear yelling. We run up to see a bunch of creatures attacking a field and a giant vine wrap around a house. Fight. After the fight a dog-faced man tips his hat towards Jack and winks. He then walks right back into the forest and disappears.

2026-06-01 (Bearskin rugs shouldn't move)

We awake from our long rest in the morning hour in our tiny hut. Mathosala approaches Tenebracanthos, asking if we saw anyone last night. Tenebracanthos says he saw a couple people and Mathosala says Martha went into the woods looking for Isaac (her son). Mathosala drops off pumpkin muffins for all of us as a gesture of good will towards helping search for Martha. Jack is only slightly conflicted. We hear people talking loudly in the square and William is yelling about how this is crazy and the town needs to rally around Martha. William approaches us and offers to come with us and help navigate the woods. As we start heading into the woods, Woody passes a perception check, but we hear druidic chanting. "From branch to soil become the oil". Tenebracanthos tries to step through the trees using an athletics check but they seem to be fighting back. The chanting gets louder. The ground rumbles as eight large trees start chanting the same phrase, "from branch to soil become the oil". Sketch makes a survival check, but he cannot find a way through. It seems like the trees are in a rave almost as the chanting continues. Jack tries to insight Martha's actions to follow what steps she may have made. Jack starts to get a feeling that we're being followed and lets the rest of the party know. Tenebracanthos uses his Tremorsense and feels that there is a creature about 30 feet away and it is on four legs, one of them with a limp. William thinks an old town used to be close to this area by Potsfield and he thinks Martha grew up in this area. Tenebracanthos tries to use an athletics check to find their way through the trees but we hear the creature's approach. They're a humanoid bat creature. We fight.

Woody and Sketch investigate the body of the dead bat creature and it seems to be one that would have hung around vampires. The creature is an evil spirit bound to a vampire corpse. William points off in the distance at a peak of a house with a candle in the window. Tenebracanthos notices a blue/green tree next to the house as we approach but they've only seen one like this a couple times before. This is an Oilwood tree. It's an ill omen and when one appears many quickly follow. Therefore people usually remove these trees as soon as they see them. This tree is specifically tied to Sister Silhouette. Sister Silhouette is best described as a hag or a witch though no one's actually seen her. Tenebracanthos does not share this info with anyone. He sees a black acrid sap from the tree and Tenebracanthos sets that tree on fire. We all smell a very decayed smell from the fire as the tree quickly becomes engulfed in flames.

As we approach the house Woody Mage Hands a knock on the front door next to the tree. We hear a sob but nothing else. Woody Mage Hands the door open as Jack enters into the foyer. As Jack continues into the house a floor plank collapses on him and the chandelier crashes down from above. He turns a corner and we see the top half of Martha attached to the bottom half of a table. She is not looking at us but she is staring at a portrait of her family. We assume this is Martha's house from her childhood. Woody smacks Martha to break her out of her trance while she's staring at the painting. Woody asks about Martha's legs and Martha just seems to realize that her legs have been replaced with a table and she screams. Martha said she saw her sister at the edge of the woods, Sydney. She died when Martha was twelve and Sydney was ten.

Potsfield was overrun by vampires and they had to leave town when Martha was young. Tenebracanthos decides to run into the next room and we see a living room with a bearskin rug. Woody's mage hand picks up the rug and as the mage hand starts to pick up the rug the bear head turns to bite the hand. Fight.

After defeating the bearskin rug Woody mockingly giggles at the voice that has been giggling at us. We walk into the dining room and we see broken chairs. Woody can tell that the giggling sound is much louder now. Woody notices that there's nothing dangerous in this room but here's creaking above us. We enter the kitchen and we see a doll on the kitchen floor. Woody uses mage hand, picks up the doll and puts it in his bag. We go back to the front of the house to go up the stairs. Tenebracanthos sees a wooden toy with a stuffed bear head on it. It runs into a room. We all look at each other and then quickly follow. We see a child's room with mini monstrosity toys. There's a doll on the bed with a knife across its lap and a ribbon around its neck. The doll turns its head towards Woody and Syd says "I'm sorry I cannot be here right now". Woody tries to remove the ribbon with mage hand and a lot of the toys animate and we go into battle.

Woody was able to find two of their friends and get them out of their charm condition in the previous battle. Woody convinces them to join us and help bring down Syd. In the center of the next room is a sewing machine with tons of various toy parts thrown about. We also see sewing needles and fabric in the closet. Sketch takes one large, two medium, three tiny silver sewing needles. We also find body parts along with Martha's legs. Jack has Detect Magic and can tell that the sewing machine and something in the other room is magical. The sewing machine being a source of magical energy is thrown from the room to the ground below. We head towards the other room and in that room we see a shadow of a girl holding a pair of scissors run out onto the balcony. Sketch and Woody chase after her being the smaller of the party members so that the floor does not break under them. As they approach the balcony, Woody goes invisible but Syd still looks directly at Woody and giggles and walks away into the woods. Jack's detect magic catches hints of the farm destroying magic in another room and they all enter. We see a 21 year old patched up man with parts of animals/things on their body. A dog-faced man is sitting in the corner and says, "Well, well, well, Syd said you'd be coming by."

Sketch proves he's not a vampire by holding silver, eating garlic, all the normal things. Brother Branch is wearing nicer clothing and seems to be a bit higher rank than the middle aged woman who is Sister Lilac. Brother Branch mentions that the orbs are new souls, not quite ready for this world. They could be recent dead or younger souls. Woody breaks down crying that he didn't know and thought they were evil things the vampires were using. Woody grovels at the feet asking if Andi is in an orb and Brother Branch says yes. He shows Woody a light blue orb. Woody touches it and is flooded with memories from Andi. Woody communicates with the orb and gets memory impressions back of how Andi died.

There's a huge effigy of an elven-like creature made from wood. There's a rotting stag at the base. The effigy looks like Tuathal. Tenebracanthos inspects it and doesn't get anything after looking at it. He sets it on fire. He hears a monotone voice from the stag. "The rot is inevitable. Not even the gods can stop the blighted." Jack throws the corpse into the fire after Tenebracanthos smashes its head. Jack creates a tiny hut for us to sleep in and we take a long rest.

We use it to get away from the nest of the bugs and navigate the forest some more. Tenebracanthos uses athletics to find good terrain.
