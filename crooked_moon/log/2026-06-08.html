Sister Rain casts two spells, one to remove the table legs and one to put her normal legs back on. Jack goes back to the area we set up the tiny hut last time to prepare it again and ensure there are no Vampires. Tenebracanthos is sketching a map. The map is pretty good, but not proportionate to where the house was. After setting up the tiny hut, Jack reads the journal from Dogz. He has notes on what he did with the details getting better as time goes on. There seems to be a rhyme within the words. "When neighbor turns on neighbor, when blood of kin is spilled, the heart is fully rotted, the curse has been fulfilled." There are a couple of orbs attached to the tree in various spots. We also notice that there are multiple orbs smashed on the ground. Sketch notices a couple bat hounds are on the roofs. We determine that these people are the trees or vampires. We need to determine how we want to approach this as Sketch starts to back away.
