2026-04-01 (I'm going to be a real Dragon)

As we continue East, Fitz sees a building of interest. We find a derelict market hall and Yormash tries to find stuff but trips a trap. Yormash then gets covered in green slime after a pot breaks on him and starts taking acid damage. Jack disarms a wasp trap and Yormash asks to no one in particular if the people setting the traps know Kanye. Fitz hears echoing of people running. Steve finishes his Detect Magic spell that he started when Yormash set off the trap, uses his tremor sense, and activates a trap which means he takes a little bit of fire damage. Yormash smothers Steve out yelling "Stop, drop and roll!" Fitz finds more traps and disarms them. We believe there's only two small to medium creatures based on Steve's tremor sense. Jack goes downstairs without telling anyone and he uses the Wand of Wonder, triggering initiative for Jack only. We hear the fireball and run after Jack. Fight.

After killing all the other Kobolds Kakarol said that Acererak will make a kobold a real dragon. It seems he's trying to talk his way out of being killed. Steve detects the potion of greater healing on Kakarol and Steve calls out that there is another magical item that isn't in his hoard. Steve intimidates him to get an obsidian skull on an iron necklace with ruby eyes and emerald teeth. Yormash heals Kakarol and Jack seems to see his hoard is about 325 gold pieces. Jack offers 125 gold pieces if he helps us find more shrines and info about Red Wizards. We start heading towards the next shrine that Kakarol pointed out. Yormash doesn't see any gargoyles on the way.

It's getting close to dinner time so we find a building for us to hunker down in. Fitz uses his Identify spell on the evil looking amulet and determines it is an Amulet of the Black Skull. It has six charges, a 100 foot teleport spell essentially. Non-undead wearers must make a DC-16 Constitution save each use. A random transformation will happen on a failure. We take a long rest.

Watch one: We hear Kobold. Then we hear the King of Feathers running. He gets the Kobold for food and teleports. Watch two: Kobold figure running perimeter of us, the rest of the watch was uneventful. Watch three: uneventful. The next morning the weather stormy. We use the jug for water.
