Tabletop campaigns

2026-06-09 (Jaculi, I barely know him)

Yormash and Fitz compare the key that opened the temple with the 6 keys on the walls. Steve Mage Hands a key into the pedestal. The statues' tendrils shoot out radiant beams damaging all of us pretty badly. Fitz puts all six keys together and it seems to make a new key. Jack twists the key into the pedestal and a Puzzle Cube emerges. We grab it and take a short rest. We head towards the Lava Lake Island. Artus, Dragonbait, and Orvex will stay behind on the main island and hide to keep our landing zone clear. Fitz ties a rope around a tree and Yormash is able to pull the knot loose pretty easily. Fitz re-ties it and the next knot is good. Yormash flies over to the island and ties the second part of the rope around a tree. Yormash flies back and then gets everyone over to the island and looks for any snakes or Yuan-ti holes. Jack is looking for movement and life on the island. It feels that something's missing but he doesn't know what it is. Jack uses Disguise Self to turn into a very tall Grung. As we approach the temple, we get attacks against us by Jaculi (snakes). We fight them.

We kill them all and see that the scales look like natural camouflage. Yormash decides to help Fitz and skins them all perfectly (natural 20). The skin can be used for Boots of Striding and Springing or a Cloak of Invisibility. We approach the temple and see the trees grow in front of the squat building and worn steps ascend to an archway that forms the entrance. A coil of serpents flank the entrance. We enter the temple of Moa and see the walls of this rubble-strewed corridor are covered with serpents and arrow slits are spaced out along them every five feet. Cobwebs fill the arrow slits and hang like drapes from the corridor ceiling. Above the entrance, a stone plaque bears a cuneiform message. We get through the corridor and see in the middle of this room, a stone cube rests on a plain stone pedestal. Statues of twelve tribal warriors jut from the surrounding walls, aiming bows at the pedestal. The floor in front of the pedestal bears a mosaic depicting a monstrous serpent constricting a furious monkey. Moss obscures much of the detail. Steve casts Scorching Ray at the moss and we see a message in old Omuan on the floor. Fitz tries to remember some words from previous markings. The first word of sentence one is Death. The first word of the second sentence is Truth. Yormash finds hidden doors while Fitz goes to give the old Omuan phrase to Orvex. We go into the hidden rooms. On the Sestern secret room that Jack went into he sees a statue of a coiled serpent dominates this web filled room. The statue is missing its head which lies broken on the floor next to a stone cube. The Eastern secret room that Yormash is going into he sees a large statue of a coiled serpent dominates this web filled room. Flushed in the statues jaws is a stone cube. As we enter the rooms Fitz gets the translation from Orvex. "Death rewards a thief deceived. Truth comes from the serpent's mouth." Fitz gets back and we hear the phrase. Jack picks up the puzzle cube in his room and tries to put it in the snake mosaic's mouth. The cube turns into green smoke and disappears. Fight. Yormash grabbed the cube in the snake's mouth from his room and ran out of the temple. The cube doesn't disappear so we know we have the right one. We use the rope bridge to get to our friends and we need to long rest.
