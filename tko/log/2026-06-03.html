2026-06-03 (3 doors down, those bards suck ass)

At the end of the last session, an arrow flies towards Jack. The beginning of this session, the arrow hits Jack and we start a battle. In the middle of the battle, we are able to convince the creature to talk. We see an elderly Tabaxi. He mentions that he is here to kill the King of Feathers. He is also looking for his siblings and son. We mentioned we are heading to the temple nearby. Steve signs us up to help kill the King of Feathers. The Tabaxi's name is Bag of Nails. We are invited into his lair through a trap door in a nearby building and discuss our lives. He is looking for the "Naval of the Moon." We trade food, water, and rations. Bag of Nails doesn't know much about the island in the Lava Lake. He saw Red Wizards trying to go there a few days ago. We head out and go towards the temple but we take a long rest in a nearby building. First watch Artus is having stomach issues and passing bad gas. We make rolls to determine if we take damage from this fart blast. Second watch, uneventful. Third watch, uneventful.

At daybreak, there is a gentle but steady rain. We debate confronting Bag of Nails, thinking he poisoned us, and decide to just go to the temple. As we approach we see swirly carvings decorate two obelisks at the entrance to the compound. An overgrown courtyard lies under the shade of a tall paltz. Stone doors steal the entrance to a windowless shrine with a smaller room standing nearby. Orvex says this appears to be the temple of Unkh the Flail Snail. The main temple doors are locked but there is a nearby shed. There are no handles or any wall to pull the door open on the shed. In old Omuan nearby Orvex reads "Unkh urges us to contrast all options before acting". Yormash and Jack push the shed door open. The roof of this workshop collapsed long ago and now thick massive plants grows from the rubble. Through the moss you see hundreds of iron keys hanging from hooks on the wall. Some are bent and broken while others are rusted beyond repair. The rest looks serviceable. Steve ritual casts Detect Magic. He doesn't detect the key based on magic, but just by noticing the key is a bit different. Steve takes the key and opens the door of the main temple. A statue of a giant snail looms before you. In place of antenna, it has five pseudopods ending in rocky clubs. Along the walls, six iron keys hang in small niches above stone plinths. Behind the statue, a carved pedestal and a wide alcove has a keyhole set into its base. As we walk in, four Ghasts lumber towards us. Fight.
