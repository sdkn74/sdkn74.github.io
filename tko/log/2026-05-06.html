After deposing of the three Yuan-ti bodies we decide to keep going in the tunnel with Fitz taking the lead. Jack decides to draw his sword and naturally a fight breaks out. During the fight a Yuan-ti bangs on the doors to alert his buddies.

After getting rid of the 5 Yuan-ti, Dragonbait lets us know that there are more coming behind the doors. Yormash heals himself while Jack heals Fitz. Steve casts Tremorsense and feels 2 medium monsters and 8 other creatures are heading to the door. As we wait a bit more Steve feels even more monsters coming so we all run out of the tunnel. We then hear screaming and yelling that we got away. We keep exploring the circular area and that's when Yormash and Fitz notice Gargoyles. We all run into a building and try to fight them without allowing them to swoop on us but eventually Yormash just goes outside to fight them and the others follow. We defeat them all prepare to rest in the building we initially tried to hide in.
