Ubtao

Description

Ubtao (pronounced: oob-TAY-oh) was the patron deity of the isolated region of Chult and indeed the Creator of Chult and Founder of Mezro. The Father of the Dinosaurs stayed distant from both mortals and other deities, and he seemed to be above the daily doings of the world and his followers. This might have been partly due to his origin as a primordial, (his alleged betrayal of them owing him his title as The Deceiver) and it remained unclear if he was even a deity in the traditional sense.

Primal Spirits

Description

Primal Spirits were incarnations of the natural world that were linked to specific natural features or locations in the Prime Material plane. They varied greatly in appearance and power. Some spirits were diminutive in size and might—often called upon for aid by travelers in wilderness―while others were venerated as semi-divine beings or even gods unto themselves.

Obo'laka

Description

Obo'laka (pronounced: oh-bah-LAH-kah) was one of the nine trickster gods of Omu in Chult. She was a primal spirit that took the form of a Zorbo. Obo'laka was obsessive, anxious, and risk-averse. She was a creature of nervous habit, and insisted on her routines.

I'jin

Description

I'jin (pronounced: EYE-jin) was one of the nine trickster gods of Omu in Chult. She was a primal spirit that took the form of an Almiraj. I'jin was fickle and unpredictable. She never stuck to a plan and even her favorite color would change constantly.

Shagambi

Description

Shagambi (pronounced: shah-GOM-bee) was one of the nine trickster gods of Omu in Chult. She was a primal spirit that took the form of a Kamadan. Shagambi was wise and virtuous, yet she showed no mercy to evildoers. Written on the wall of a hut entering Omu "Shagambi = Wisdom".

Nangnang

Description

Nangnang (pronounced: NANG-nang) was one of the nine trickster gods of Omu in Chult. She was a primal spirit that took the form of a Grung. Among some of her followers, she had once been venerated as a goddess of death.

Papazotl

Description

Papazotl (pronounced: pah-pah-ZAH-tul) was one of the nine trickster gods of Omu in Chult. He was a primal spirit that took the form of an Eblis. Papazotl was conniving, shrewd, and imperious. He refused to listen to others, yet expected them to obey him.

Kubazan

Description

Kubazan (pronounced: KOO-bah-zahn) was one of the nine trickster gods of Omu in Chult. He was a primal spirit that took the form of a Froghemoth. Kubazan was wild, free-spirited, and adventurous. He was unafraid to take great risks. Written on the wall of a hut entering Omu "Kubazan = Bravery".

Wongo

Description

Wongo (pronounced: WONG-go) was one of the nine trickster gods of Omu in Chult. He was a primal spirit that took the form of a Su-Monster. Wongo was deranged, violent, and egocentric. He had no concern for the well-being of others.

Moa

Description

Moa (pronounced: MOH-ah) was one of the nine trickster gods of Omu in Chult. He was a primal spirit that took the form of a Jaculi. Moa was kind and honest. He always spoke the truth. Written on the wall of a hut entering Omu "Moa = ?".

Unkh

Description

Unkh (pronounced: UNK) was one of the nine trickster gods of Omu in Chult. She was a primal spirit that took the form of a Flail Snail. Unkh was indecisive, self-absorbed, and incapable of making decisions, yet she was also contemplative and understanding.
